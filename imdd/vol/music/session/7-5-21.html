Dryftwoods session details


July 5, 2021
Austin's House - Clarkston, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); RKeys (v, el-p); Steve (v); JT (v, perc); Austin (el-p, b); Cameron (el-p, d); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (stereo mics center of room) captured on a Zoom H4n field recorder directly to SD card.

This is the first recording session where separate microphones were used to capture the drums, however, the other amplified instruments and vocals were still captured with a pair of microphones placed generally in the middle of the room.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 1:00 p.m. and concluded at 7:30 p.m.
