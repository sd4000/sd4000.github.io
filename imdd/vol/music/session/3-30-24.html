Dryftwoods session details


March 30, 2024
Ben Hameen's House
Mixed by Ben Hameen

Release Notes:

This session was originally released as Runnin with the team on digital format.


Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g, el-p); Joey Strings (b, el-p); RKeys (v, el-p); Max (d); JT (v, perc)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (mics on vocals/drums, instruments direct) captured to Zoom F6 field recorder to SD card via the QSC Touchmix-30 auxiliary outs. Additionally, audio (mics of vocals/drums, instruments direct) captured to QSC TouchMix-30 directly to solid state drive.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 1:00 p.m. and concluded at 6:00 p.m.
