Dryftwoods session details


July 31, 2022
Dave's House (carport) - Clarkston, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); RKeys (v, el-p, d); JT (v, perc); Raw D (v); T-Bird (v); Dwan (as); Austin (el-p, o, b); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. The QSC Touchmix-30 was used as soundboard. Audio (mics center of room) was captured on the Zoom F6 field recorder to SD card.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 2:30 p.m. and concluded at 10:00 p.m.
