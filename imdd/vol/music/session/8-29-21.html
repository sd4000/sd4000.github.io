Dryftwoods session details


August 29, 2021
Dave's House (carport) - Lakeside, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); RKeys (v, el-p); Raw D (v); T-Bird (v); JT (v, perc); Maru (g, b); Austin (el-p); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d); Cameron (d)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (bass direct, mics on drums, mics center of room) captured to a Zoom F6 field recorder. Audio also (stereo mics center of room) captured on a Zoom H4n field recorder directly to SD card. Additionally, audio (mic center of room) was captured on a phone recorder for some of the tracks.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 12:30 p.m. and concluded at 7:30 p.m.
