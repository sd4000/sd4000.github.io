Dryftwoods session details


June 5, 2021
Dave's House (carport) - Lakeside, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); RKeys (v, el-p); Steve (v); Raw D (v); Adam (g); Aaron (g); Austin (el-p); Joey Strings (b); Max (d)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (stereo mic center of room) captured on a Zoom H4n field recorder directly to SD card. The QSC Touchmix-30 was used as soundboard.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 2:00 p.m. and concluded at 12:00 a.m.
