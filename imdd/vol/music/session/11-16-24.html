Dryftwoods session details


November 16, 2024
JT's House (basement) - Decatur, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Release Notes:

This session was originally released as Birthday Bash feat MC Xavi on digital format.


Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); Xavi (v); RKeys (v, el-p); Austin (el-p, b); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (mics on vocals, instruments direct, no mics on drums) captured to QSC TouchMix-30 directly to solid state drive. Additionally, audio (mic center of room) captured to a phone recorder.

Mixed on Logic Pro.

Recording session began at 6:00 p.m. and concluded at 11:30 p.m.
