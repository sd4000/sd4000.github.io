Dryftwoods session details


January 28, 2022
Austin's House - Clarkston, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); RKeys (v, el-p); JT (v, perc, d); Austin (el-p, o, b); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (mics on speakers) captured to a Zoom F6 field recorder. Additionally, audio (stereo mics center of room) captured on a Zoom H4n field recorder directly to SD card.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 6:00 p.m. and concluded at 11:00 p.m.
