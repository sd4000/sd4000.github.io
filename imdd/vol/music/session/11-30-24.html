Max's House (downstairs) - Kirkwood, GA
Mixed by Ben Hameen

Release Notes:

This session was originally released as The Maxstravaganza on digital format.


Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); RKeys (v, el-p, d); Steve (v); Xavi (v); Raw D (v); Thorn (v); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Austin (el-p, b); Max (d); JT (perc)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (mics of vocals/drums, instruments direct) captured to QSC TouchMix-30 directly to solid state drive.

Mixed on the QSC Touchmix-30.

Recording session began at 4:00 p.m. and concluded at 10:30 p.m.
