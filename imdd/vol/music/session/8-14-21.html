Dryftwoods session details


August 14, 2021
Austin's House - Clarkston, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:
Ben Hameen (v, g); RKeys (v, el-p, d); Steve (v, perc); JT (v, perc); Aaron (g); Austin (el-p, b); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d); Cameron (d)

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (mics on drums, mics center of room) captured to a Zoom F6 field recorder. Additionally, audio (stereo mics center of room) captured on a Zoom H4n field recorder directly to SD card.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 2:00 p.m. and concluded at 9:30 p.m.
