April 2, 2024

Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g, el-p, b); RKeys (v, el-p, d); JT (v); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d, el-p)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (mics on vocals/drums, instruments direct) captured to Zoom F6 field recorder to SD card via the QSC Touchmix-30 auxiliary outs.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 7:30 p.m. and concluded at 11:00 p.m.
