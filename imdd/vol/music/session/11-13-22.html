Dryftwoods session details


November 13, 2022
Ben Hameen's House - Decatur, GA
Mixed by Joey Strings

Session Musicians:
Ben Hameen (v, g); JT (v, perc); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Austin (el-p, o, b); Max (d)

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (6-channels) captured to a Zoom F6 field recorder via the QSC TouchMix-30 directly to SD card.

Mixed on Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 2:30 p.m. and concluded at 6:30 p.m.
