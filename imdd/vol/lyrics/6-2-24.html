Dryftwoods session details


June 2, 2024
Ben Hameen's House - Decatur, GA
Mixed by Ben Hameen and Joey Strings

Release Notes:

This session was originally released as Tellin Stories on digital format.


Session Musicians:

Ben Hameen (v, g); Joey Strings (b, el-p); Max (d)


Session Notes:

This session was performed and recorded live. Audio (mics of vocals/drums, instruments direct) captured to QSC TouchMix-30 directly to solid state drive. Additionally, audio (mics on vocals/drums, instruments direct) captured to Zoom F6 field recorder to SD card via the QSC Touchmix-30 auxiliary outs. There were two final mixes, Zoom F6 channels were mixed on Final Cut Pro, and the QSC Touchmix-30 channels were mixed on the QSC Touchmix-30.

Mixed on the QSC Touchmix-30 and Final Cut Pro.

Recording session began at 4:30 p.m. and concluded at 10:30 p.m.
